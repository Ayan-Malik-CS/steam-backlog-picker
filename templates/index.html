📖 About This Project

What Should I Play? is a free tool to help you organize and discover games in your Steam backlog.

  • Filter by playtime: Focus on untouched games or games you've already started
  • Sort by length: See how long games take via HowLongToBeat integration
  • Hide free-to-play: Filter out F2P games from your collection
  • Game picker modes: Use Spin the Wheel, Versus Mode, or Mystery Box to pick your next game

⚠️ Note: This is a solo hobby project deployed for fun with no commercial backing. Expect slow updates and occasional downtime. All features are free and always will be.

🔒 Privacy & Data

  • Your data is not sold: We only fetch and store your Steam library locally for this session
  • No tracking: We use optional Google Analytics to track page views only
  • Session-based: Each Steam ID has its own independent library view
  • Safe to use: We never post to your Steam account or modify anything

❓ Troubleshooting

  • Profile is private? Go to Steam Settings → Privacy and set your library to "Public"
  • No HLTB times showing? Click "Sync" under HLTB Times (may take 1-2 minutes)
  • Free games not detecting? Click "Sync" under Free Games to scan your library
  • Invalid Steam ID? Enter your custom URL name (username) or your 17-digit Steam ID
  • Site is slow? This is a free-tier Render app - it may be sleeping. First request takes 30 seconds to wake up
