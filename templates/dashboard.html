❓ Help & Troubleshooting

Common Issues:

❌ "Profile is private"

Go to Steam → Settings → Privacy and set your library to "Public"

❌ No HLTB times showing

Click "Sync" under HLTB Times. First sync may take 2-3 minutes. Not all games have data on HowLongToBeat (mainly indie/niche games).

❌ Free games not detecting

Click "Sync" under Free Games. This queries Steam for each game - can be slow on large libraries (100+ games).

❌ Genres loading slowly

Genre sync queries Steam for EACH game. On a 300+ game library, this takes 5-10 minutes. Be patient!

❌ Invalid Steam ID error

Use your Steam username/custom URL (e.g., "GabeLoganNewell") OR your 17-digit Steam ID.

❌ Site is very slow or not loading

⚠️ This is running on Render's free tier. Free instances go to sleep after 15 minutes of inactivity. Your first request takes 30+ seconds to wake up. If there's too much traffic, the site may slow down or temporarily stop working. This is a limitation of free hosting.

✅ How do I hide games without HLTB times?

Under Filters, check "Hide games without playtime data". This filters out any game without a recorded HLTB time.

✅ Can I change my Steam ID?

Click "Change User" button to logout and enter a different Steam ID or username.

About This Project:

This is a free solo hobby project with no commercial backing. Hosted on Render's free tier, so expect occasional slowdowns during high traffic. All features are free and always will be. If you find a bug, use the bug report form on the home page!
